About JW Player

The JW Player is the world's most popular HTML5/Flash embeddable media player for websites. It supports a wide array of (mobile) platforms and media formats. It is open-source and easy to configure, customize and extend.

Browser & Media Support

Since the JW Player leverages both HTML5 <video> and Adobe Flash, it supports a wider range of browsers/devices and audio/video formats than is possible with HTML5 or Flash alone. Here is an overview:

Browser Video Audio YouTube Streaming
Internet Explorer MP4, FLV AAC, MP3 Yes HLS, RTMP
Chrome MP4, FLV, WebM AAC, MP3, Vorbis Yes HLS, RTMP
Firefox MP4, FLV, WebM AAC, MP3 Yes HLS, RTMP
Safari MP4, FLV AAC, MP3 Yes HLS, RTMP
iOS MP4 AAC, MP3 Yes 1 HLS
Android MP4, WebM 2 AAC, MP3, Vorbis 2 - HLS 2
  1. Limited support leveraging the player fallback option.
  2. WebM video, Vorbis audio and HLS streams are supported as of Android 4.0

JW Player automatically selects whether to use HTML5 or Flash based upon the media format(s) you embed and the browser or device a visitor uses to view your site.

Feature Overview

JW Player is a mature product with a complete and robust set of features:

  • A slick and intuitive interface that looks and works the same across desktop browsers and across mobile devices.
  • An automated and elegant model for selecting HTML5 or Flash for rendering the media.
  • An extensive list of options for easily configuring layout and behavior of the player.
  • Comprehensive playlist support for loading multiple media items, file formats and quality levels.
  • Display of captions or subtitles using MP4 text tracks or external SRT/DFXP files.

JW Player can be customized and extended beyond its existing feature set. A Skinning Model is available to completely match the player's look and feel to a website and a JavaScript API is available to add new functionalities or build rich player-page interactions.

Advanced Features

JW Player includes support for many advanced use cases not commonly found in other players:

  • Live and adaptive streaming through the Apple HLS and Adobe RTMP protocols.
  • Analytics callbacks to Google Analytics or any custom analytics backend.
  • Content discovery through the display of Related Videos or Social Sharing options.
  • In-stream advertising using the Vast/VPaid standards or Self Hosted Ads.

Extensive integrations with the popular Drupal CMS and WordPress CMS are available. They enable easy setup of JW Player and embedding of audio/video content into your site.
